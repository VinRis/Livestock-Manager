# **App Name**: Livestock Lynx

## Core Features:

- Livestock Profiles: Detailed profiles for each animal, tracking breed, birth date, health records, and production metrics.
- Farm Activity Log: Daily log for recording feeding, breeding, health treatments, and other farm activities.
- Financial Dashboard: Manage income and expenses related to livestock, providing insights into profitability.
- Analytics & Reporting: Generate reports on livestock production metrics like milk yield, weight gain, and breeding success, and suggests opportunities to increase profit and animal wellness.
- Mobile-First Design: The app is designed for mobile use, ensuring accessibility and ease of use in the field. Bottom navigation bar for one-thumb use.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Glowy green (#A7F3D0) to evoke freshness and growth, mirroring agricultural vitality.
- Background color: Natural grey (#F3F4F6) for a grounded, earthy feel that doesn't distract from the content.
- Accent color: Deeper green (#34D399) for key actions and highlights, ensuring important elements stand out.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for a blend of modern and warm readability, fitting for both detailed profiles and quick logs.
- Use custom icons representing livestock types, farm activities, and financial metrics. Consistent style and sizing.
- Mobile-first layout with a bottom navigation bar for quick access to key features. Clean and intuitive design.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience. For example, smooth loading of livestock profiles or chart updates.
